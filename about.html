About me

Hey there, I'm Paul - a structural engineer by day and a cloud computing/DevOps enthusiast by night. I'm always eager to learn about the latest tech trends and how they can be leveraged to solve complex business problems.

Computer science was my first career passion back in high school, but life took me on a different path. Many years later, I've decided to give my old passion a go and pursue a career change into the world of cloud computing and DevOps.

I'm also a devoted family man, married with a little one who's turning three soon. Being a dad has taught me the true meaning of patience, resilience, and the joy of watching a little one grow up before your very eyes.

So, when I'm not learning about the latest tech trends, you can find me exploring the great outdoors with my wife and daughter. We love exploring the Australian coastline and bush as well as hitting the ski slopes in new and exciting places around the world.

One can also often find me in the gym, lifting heavy weights. It's not just about physical health for me, but also about the mental benefits of discipline and motivation. Pushing myself to reach new goals, even on the days when I don't feel like it, helped me develop the kind of grit and determination that I bring to all aspects of my life. Whether it's in the gym or in my career, I'm always striving to be my best and push beyond my limits.

I'm a firm believer in lifelong learning and staying on the cutting edge of technology. With my engineering background and passion for tech, I'm excited to build a career in cloud computing and DevOps that allows me to make a real impact in the industry. Let's connect and see how we can collaborate to bring your tech projects to the next level!
